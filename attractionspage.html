Golfing

Carlsbad has multiple state of the art golf courses throughout the town. It is also home to various tournaments throughout the year. Check out these courses and schedule your next tee time next time you visit Carlsbad! Here are my recommendations:

The Crossings at Carlsbad offers well-manicured courses near many attractions in Carlsbad.
Rancho Carlsbad Golf Club offers affordable and high quality golfing off of El Camino Real.
Golf at Omni La Costa is equipped with a full-service resort and spa.
Aviara Golf Club overlooks the Batiquitos Coastal reserve and has scenic views.

Carlsbad Flower Fields

The Carlsbad Flower Fields attracts visitors from far and wide when the expansive fields of flowers are in bloom. Spring time is when the fields are in their full bloom, and they last a few weeks before the flowers begin to die off. Visitors have to buy tickets into the fields, but the memories and photos are often worth the price. Locals know this as a very touristy destination, and many have never actually paid the price to get into the fields; rather they view the beauty of the fields as they drive by. You can take tours of the fields and attend the various events they hold (check out their website for more information). The Flower Fields is a popular spot for weddings, photo shoots, and various other gatherings. The Flower Fields are located directly next to the Carlsbad Premium outlets, close to Legoland, and a short drive to the beach.

Carlsbad Farmer's Market

Every Wednesday from 3pm - 7pm, a part of State Street in the village of Carlsbad shuts down to turn into a farmer's market. Find fresh vegetables, flowers, local vendors and live music. Come to enjoy an attraction that is less touristy in Carlsbad. Parking can be tricky during busy times, but there are various free lots and street parking around Carlsbad. A great quality of Carlsbad is the ample free parking around. The farmer's market is always a fun time with friends and family.

Agua Hedionda Lagoon

Address: 4215 Harrison Street Carlsbad, California 92008
The Carlsbad Lagoon is located on the other side of the 5 freeway away from the ocean. It is home to jet ski rentals, stand-up paddle boarding, and more! Locals and visitors can take their boats out onto the glassy waters to water ski and wake board. "The lagoon" as the locals call it is a popular hangout spot for kids and adults. Check out Carlsbad Lagoon's website for more information on rentals and more!

LEGOLAND California & Sea Life Aquarium

LEGOLAND California attracts thousands of visitors every month as it is one of two Legolands in the United States. LEGOLAND California is home to Legoland Resort, Sea Life Aquarium, and the Legoland Waterpark. Visiting all the Legoland attractions can be a multiple day event, and you can book a stay in the Legoland hotel as you complete all the Lego activites.
Check out the PARK MAP here!New attractions are added every year!
Visit Legoland's website for some tips & tricks and other useful information.
Coming from a Carlsbad local and former Legoland worker, I recommend the Technic coaster and the Dragon coaster, as well as attending the waterpark during the hotter days!

Carlsbad Village Street Fair

The Carlsbad Village Street Fair is a bi-annual event that is known as "North County's favorite street fair". Various streets shut down on two Sundays a year and welcome over 800 vendors. Tasty food, unique collectables, handmade gifts, and fun and games for the family can be found in the Carlsbad Street Fair. Many locals have a tradition to attend the fair every year.he fair stretches 14 blocks. Grand Avenue from Carlsbad Boulevard to Jefferson Street, and all of the cross streets in between, would be close for the event. Each year, the Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce hosts the fair in May and November. Early birds get there when it opens at 8am. The fair usually goes until 4pm. Parking is often scarce during these days and it is best to be dropped off.

Surf Lessons

Ever wanted to learn how to surf but don't have a surfboard? Don't have a wetsuit? Are afraid? Then you should sign up for a surf lesson while in Carlsbad! Carlsbad is home to many great surfers and it would be the perfect opportunity to learn from a pro! Here are some options for you to checkout and compare:

So Cal Surf Lessons offers private lessons as well as surf camps. Located at Warm Waters Jetty and Robert Frazee State Beach.
Surfin' Fire Surf School offers private lessons, group lessons, and surf camps.

Shopping

Carlsbad has multiple outdoor and indoor shopping complexes that will satisfy any shopaholic's dream. The Carlsbad Premium Outlets have many high-end brands at an "outlet" price point. Shop Kate Spade, Calvin Klein, Nike, and more! This complex is an open-air mall with various events throughout the year. The Shoppes at Carlsbad is a newly renovated indoor shopping center with many popular stores and delicious restaurants. A 24 Hour Fitness is also attached to the complex as well as a state of the art movie theater. The Forum is a high-end open-air shopping complex located in La Costa on the border of Carlsbad and Encinitas. Come to shop at places like Anthropologie, Lululemon, and Apple. Enjoy various eateries and a good vibes.
