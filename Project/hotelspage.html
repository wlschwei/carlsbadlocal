Where to Stay in Carlsbad, CA

• • •

From beachside resorts to hotels with golf course views, Carlsbad offers many places to stay for visitors of all types.
Here are my recommendations:

Grand Pacific Palisades Resort & Hotel

Price: $$
The Grand Pacific Palisades is a timeshare rental resort that offers luxorious pools, views of the beach and overlooking the flower fields, and has a great proximity to Legoland and other attractions.

Tamarack Beach Resort & Hotel

Price: $$
The Tamarack Beach resort is steps away from the beach! It is a short walk away from downtown Carlsbad and many restaurants. Stay here for a beachside stay and local feel.

Sheraton Carlsbad Resort & Spa

Price: $$$
The Sheraton Resort & Spa is some of the finest accomodations you will find in Carlsbad. The pools are state of the art and the suites are set up as villas. The Sheraton is in close proximity to Legoland.

Beach Terrace Inn

Price: $$$
The Beach Terrace Inn is as close to sleeping on the beach as it gets. Located right on the sand, this resort offers a pool with views of the ocean and a private beach. Perfect for anyone looking to live the beach life.

Carlsbad Inn Beach Resort

Price: $$
The Carlsbad Inn Beach Resort is located in the middle of the village, with unique eateries behind it and the ocean in front of it. Stay steps away from the beach while immersing yourself in the heart of Carlsbad.

Legoland Hotel

Price: $$$
The Legoland Hotel is perfect for families looking to gain the full Lego experience. Stay in a themed room and allow your child to live out their imagination! Steps away from the theme park and other attractions in Carlsbad.
