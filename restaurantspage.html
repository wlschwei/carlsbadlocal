Best Places to Eat in
Carlsbad, California

• • •

Carlsbad offers a wide variety of food, from craft breweries to fine dining steakhouses with oceanfront views, I guarantee you won't go hungry when visting Carlsbad.

Norte Mexican Food and Cocktails

Norte Mexican Food is an iconic spot in Carlsbad, It has been around for ages and is family-owned. Norte offers excellent traditional Mexican food at reasonable prices. They have a fun and lively atmosphere and beautiful outdoor seating areas. Come for Taco Tuesday for the best $2 tacos in town. Norte is located right next to a main beach access, so after your meal walk over and catch the sunset! They offer parking in the Carlsbad Inn Parking garage right under the restaurant. Come hungry and enjoy free chips and salsa!

Pizza Port

Pizza Port Brewing Company is an iconic pizza place in Carlsbad and throughout Southern California. Pizza Port Carlsbad has been around for more than 20 years and gives customers great food, brews, and memories. Pizza Port brews local beers and serves craft pizzas along with delicious other items the whole family will love. Its location in Carlsbad Village makes it easy to walk to other places in Carlsbad or catch the sunset on the beach after dinner. I recommend the lunch special!

Park 101

Park 101 is a new spot in Carlsbad that has quickly gained popularity since its opening. Its unique layout and menu items give customers a great experience for all members of the family. CHeck out their website for monthly events such as trivia, paint night, and more! Come for brunch and grab an acai bowl or dress up for an outdoor dinner and drinks with friends. Bring the kids for games like corn hole while you choose from 1 of 32 craft brews. I recommend "The Chick" sandwich!

Campfire

Campfire is unlike any other restaurant you may encounter. The campfire theme of the restaurant is prevelant through the smell of a campfire throughout the restaurant and the decor to match. The employees dress to match the theme as well! Their menu offerings are unique and very tasty, however are more on the pricier side. I recommend making a reservation as the popularity of the restaurant make for busy nights. Located on the outskirts of Carlsbad Village, you will find ample parking.

Village Kitchen & Pie Shoppe

Carlsbad Village Kitchen and Pie Shoppe is a classic Carlsbad diner that all the locals know as a place with friendly people and great food. Be careful going on Sundays as it is extremely busy. Village Kitchen is known for their delicious selection of pies, and the locals all have their favorites (mine's the chocolate coconut!). Their pies are extremely popular during the holiday season! I recommend their belgian waffle for brunch!
